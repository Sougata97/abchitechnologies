Rajarshi Chanda

Advisor & Mentor

Business Strategy | Brand Promotion | Business Analytics

Mr. Rajarshi Chanda, popularly known as Raj among his friends & fans, is a Data Scientist, Business Analyst and successful Entrepreneur, who feels that he is blessed for what he has achieved in his life. Now he sincerely wants to put some of his valuable time and effort to help those younger Entrepreneurs who want to follow his path and join his league of excellence. He accepted this path as a way to return something back to the society out of gratitude. He has spent around three decades serving in a number of reputed IT Product MNCs and Global Blue Chips, mostly working as a Sr. Data Analysts.

He built & led many teams for leading Data Science & Analytics projects in the areas of Natural Language Processing, Image Based Search, Business Analytics & Demand Forecasting. He always achieved respect of his teams and seniors for his knowledge across different technological domains and business functions. He led his teams with an example, at the same time being a team-man. His famous saying- “be strict about the policies and sympathized about the people” is a guiding principle for many young managers and entrepreneurs. Since 2016, he is chasing his dream of becoming an entrepreneur. He has co-founded a Bangalore based IT Product Company and is working on some revolutionary business ideas that will change our society for once and all. He has written his own success story becoming an eminent Entrepreneur. Now it is time to help other entrepreneurs to build their success stories. He completed his B.E. in Electronics & Telecommunication Engg. and M.B.A. from IIM Ahmedabad. But he believes that knowledge can be acquired from anyone and anywhere. He remains always up-to-date in modern technologies and business methods by continuous knowledge sharing with his peers spread across the globe.
