About Abchi Technologies Pvt. Ltd.:

Abchi Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (popularly known as “Abchi”) was incorporated in 2021 by two technical professionals who are friends from their engineering college days, but have come from very different professional backgrounds. After completion of study in Electronics & Communication Engineering, one of the founders of the Company pursued jobs in a few reputed companies before starting his own venture in 2010. Since then, he has proven his mettle in entrepreneurship by successfully creating a leading brand in e-Governance & IT Services Sectors. He has earned many feathers on his hat by successfully executing a number of technically challenging projects in geographically difficult locations spread across Eastern India & North-East. He has served for many State Governments & reputed MNCs in India. The other founder dedicated his life in technical innovation in the field of Computer Science & IT. After completion of B.Tech in IT and M.E. in Multimedia Technology from Jadavpur University, he followed his passion in technically challenging jobs. He has got access to all modern technologies while working for a leading MNC of this era. He has gained ample knowledge & expertise while developing applications for a number of internationally reputed clients from overseas. His approach is always goal-oriented with optimal client satisfaction. Although they belong to very different professional domains, their common

motivation to dedicate their lives for IT Revolutions in coming days helped them to join their hands for this aspirational venture. They understood that Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Internet of Things (Sensor Networks) coupled with Cloud Computing shall reshape the future world. And that will be very different from today’s world. They aspire to be a catalyst to this transformation of the world. Since its inception, Abchi has dedicated all its resources to build an intelligent network around the consumers and manufacturers. As the first step, Abchi has built an e-commerce platform to serve the consumers from the bottom of the pyramid, residing at Tier B & C Cities along with Sub-urban & Rural Areas. For suppliers, Abchi has maintained a fine balance between internationally reputed premier brands and local manufacturers of quality products. Especially, for Raw, Prepared & Semi-prepared food market in addition to Grocery & Handicraft market, Abchi adopted the policy of promoting local vendors in line with “Vocal for Local” initiative taken by the Government of India. Abchi has a recruitment policy favouring locals for employment as a measure to create jobs at very grassroots level, further developing rural economy. Abchi also gives preference to Ex-Service Personnel (who retired early from Indian Army, Navy or Air Force) offering them an opportunity for re-employment. Abchi salutes those brave sons/daughters of India for their services and sacrifices for this great nation. No country can prosper without the sacrifices made by these heroes. Abchi feels that this is our duty to repay our debt with gratitude. Abchi has just taken a baby step, but it has a vision for better tomorrow. Some big companies today may fail to fit to the need of future world, whereas some small enterprises shall write new success stories. Abchi aspires to be recognized as the greatest catalyst for the technological revolutions ahead. A baby but mighty step has been taken by us towards the future…………………………………..Our Future.
